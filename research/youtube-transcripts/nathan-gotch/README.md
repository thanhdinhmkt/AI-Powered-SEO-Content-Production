## Channel Intelligence

**Background:**
Nathan Gotch is the co-founder of Rankability (an AI SEO platform), author of *AI SEO For Dummies*, and founder of Search OS. He has trained tens of thousands of SEO practitioners through his "SEO Academy" and is known for making technical SEO accessible without dumbing it down.

**Core research areas documented on his channel:**

**1. GEO/SEO Integration**
Nathan's framework: "GEO doesn't replace SEO — it builds on it." His content explicitly maps which traditional SEO practices transfer directly to AI visibility and which require new thinking. Key principle: site health, technical hygiene, structured data, and clear content remain foundational.

**2. AI Keyword Clustering**
Deep-dive tutorials on using AI models to group keywords by semantic intent rather than lexical similarity. His workflow: export keywords → pass through embedding model → cluster by cosine similarity → assign to content hubs.

**3. Internal Linking Automation**
Documented workflow using AI to analyze site structure and recommend internal link insertions based on topical proximity and page authority signals.

**4. Content for AI Citations**
His framework for "cite-ready" content:
- Add dates, numbers, and definitions to every major claim
- Create a "what changed / what to do next" section in every article
- Use primary sources, not aggregators
- Clear URL structures that reflect topic intent

**5. Programmatic + Parasite + AI Hybrid Systems**
Published a framework combining: aged domains with backlink profiles, parasite pages for high-competition keywords, and programmatic AI sites for long-tail — with CTR/user-signal optimization layered on top.

**Key quote (from nathangotch.com, March 2026):**
> "The easiest way to rank in 2025? Don't launch a site. Launch a system."
