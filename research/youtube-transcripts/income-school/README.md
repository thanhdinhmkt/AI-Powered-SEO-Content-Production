## Channel Intelligence

**Why Income School is a unique data source:**

Most SEO practitioners optimize for ranking velocity — what gets traffic fastest. Income School optimizes for **sustainable revenue over 18–36 months**, which makes their data far more valuable for understanding AI content's long-term effects.

**The Project 24 methodology:**
- Build a niche content site from zero
- No backlink building — organic authority only
- Publish 30–60 articles in a tight niche
- Monetize via display ads (Mediavine/AdThrive) + affiliate
- Target: replace a full-time income within 24 months

This no-backlink approach provides a **clean control variable** for isolating content quality effects in AI search — the only variable being content itself.

**Key research questions their recent content addresses:**

**1. AI Content & Long-Term Ad Revenue**
What happens to RPM and session duration 12 months after a site shifts from human-written to AI-generated content? Income School tracks this with real revenue data.

**2. Helpful Content Update (HCU) Recovery**
Sites hit by HCU in 2023–2024 — which recovered, which didn't, and what distinguishes them. Important for understanding what "helpful" actually means algorithmically.

**3. Niche Site Viability in AI Search Era**
Can a small niche site survive when AI Overviews answer questions directly? Income School's longitudinal data (sites running 3–5+ years) is the best available evidence.

**4. Content Volume vs. Content Depth Trade-offs**
Their experiments compare: 100 thin articles vs. 30 comprehensive articles vs. 50 medium articles — with revenue data attached.

**Format:**
Videos are typically 15–40 minutes, conversational format between Ricky and Jim Harmer. Transcripts will be long but contain high-value longitudinal data and candid assessments of what's working and what isn't.
